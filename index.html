Classic Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 1 small red onion
  • 1-2 fresh red chillies
  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 bunch of fresh coriander
  • 6 ripe cherry tomatoes
  • 2 limes
  • 2 limes

Method

  1. Peel the onion and deseed 1 chilli, then roughly chop it all on a large board.
  2. Destone the avocados and scoop the flesh onto the board.
  3. Start chopping it all together until fine and well combined.
  4. Pick over most of the coriander leaves, roughly chop and add the tomatoes, then continue chopping it all together.
  5. Deseed, finely chop and scatter over the remaining chilli if you like more of a kick.
  6. Pick over the reserved coriander leaves, then serve. tip: Super-quick and easy, this guacamole recipe is delicious with fajitas, quesadillas, dolloped into a wrap or served as a snack with crunchy veggies.
